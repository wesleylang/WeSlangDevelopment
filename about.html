We Slang Development

"We started as a joke"

We Slang Development started as a group of friends joking around about one day getting together and building a company together. As the years have gone by, the joke stuck in the back of our minds and soon became a hope. A hope and eventually a dream. Now that dream has become a reality in the form of We Slang Development.

We Slang Development is a play on words on one of the founder's name, Wesley Lang. The play on words also takes from modern slang to mean "We sell development".
